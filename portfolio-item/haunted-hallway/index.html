Lighting Project 03

I spent many years obsessing over horror movies. I finally got to see how much work went into them while lighting just this one scene. I learned that it’s ok to have a dark scene as long as you have a reason for it. There are questions I asked myself through out the project. How can I lead their eyes with lighting? What do I want them to see first? Honestly, it was surprisingly quick to complete. This project really opened up my eyes about how important good lighting can help bring a scene to life.
