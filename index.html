ABOUT ME

I love doodling. Thanks to my supportive parents, my childhood consisted of a wide range of activities from Taekwondo, ballet, piano lessons, and art classes. However, the only one that stuck with me was art.

Currently I’m a digital designer at Doe Anderson. If I had to describe the best part about my job, it would be the satisfaction of using art to convey stories. Some people call it advertising, I call it the art of persuasion.
